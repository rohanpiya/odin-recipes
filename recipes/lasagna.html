Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked pasta dish made by layering pasta sheets with a rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese mixture, and melted mozzarella cheese. The layers are baked together until hot, bubbly, and golden on top.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb (450 g) ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 oz (680 g) tomato sauce or pasta sauce
  • 15 oz (425 g) ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions and drain.
  3. In a large pan, cook the onion and garlic until soft. Add the meat and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in the tomato sauce, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, egg, and Parmesan cheese.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a baking dish.
  7. Add a layer of noodles, then ricotta mixture, meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese.
  8. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with sauce and mozzarella on top.
  9. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  10. Remove the foil and bake for another 20–25 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  11. Let the lasagna rest for 10–15 minutes before slicing and serving.
